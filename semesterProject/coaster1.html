Coaster #1 - Steel Vengeance

Steel Vengeance was manufactured by Rocky Mountain Construction and is considered by many fellow enthusiasts to be the best roller coaster on the planet. This coaster is a rare hybrid type with a wooden support structure mixed with steel track. The coaster opened with many structural problems due to new heavy prototype trains and a 200 ft wooden structure. Both the park and manufacturer are working endlessly to resolve the issue. With a 200 ft, 90 degree vertical drop and more than 30 seconds of combined airtime, it is no doubt an exciting experience and a must do bucket list coaster for people who enjoy roller coasters.

Coaster Details

  • Name: Steel Vengeance
  • Location: Cedar Point in Sandusky, Ohio
  • Make: Rocky Mountain Construction
  • Model: IBox track / custom layout
  • Opened: 2018
  • Height: 205 ft
  • Length: 5,740 ft
  • Max Speed: 74 mph
  • Inversions: 4
  • Angle of Decent: 90.0°
  • Duration: 2:30
