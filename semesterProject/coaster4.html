Coaster #4 - Fury 325

Fury 325 is a world class coaster that opened in 2015 by Bolliger and Mabillard or B&M for short. This coaster opened as the longest steel roller coaster in north America and the ride crosses over the border between north and south Carolina multiple times. The ride also passes under a pedestrian bridge giving incredible views of the ride and extreme intimidation. This coaster won the golden ticket award for best new ride of 2015 and ever since then has won best overall steel coaster from 2016 â€“ 2023. In 2023 however one of the support beams on the ride had a crack in it which took the news by storm. Although with this crack the riders were not in immediate danger and the ride was closed almost immediately afterwards. This ride is definitely one of all-time best and is well deserved of being in the top 5 in the world.

Coaster Details

  • Name: Fury 325
  • Location: Carowids in Charlotte, NC
  • Make: Bolliger & Mabillard

  • Height: 325 ft
  • Length: 6,602 ft
