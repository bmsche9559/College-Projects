Coaster #2 - Iron Gwazi

Iron Gwazi is the younger brother of steel vengeance and is considered just as good, if not better than its older brother. Iron Gwazi is a renovation of Busch Gardens old dueling wooden coaster named Gwazi. Rocky Mountain Construction used their same remodeling technique from Steel Vengeance on this hybrid behemoth coaster. This coaster features a similar height and drop to its brother only slightly shorter and half of the inversions. Although this coaster hardly needs extra inversions as it focuses more on speed and airtime. This is once again a bucket list coaster and shows why RMC is not to be messed with.

Coaster Details

  • Name: Iron Gwazi
  • Location: Busch Gardens Tampa in Tampa, Florida
  • Make: Rocky Mountain Construction
  • Model: IBox track / custom layout
  • Opened: 2022
  • Height: 206 ft
  • Length: 4,075 ft
  • Max Speed: 76 mph
  • Inversions: 2
  • Angle of Decent: 91.0°
  • Duration: 1:50
