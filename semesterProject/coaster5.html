Coaster #5 - Kingda ka

Kingda Ka is the world’s tallest and 2nd fastest roller coaster on the planet and built by Intamin. You may be thinking why this coaster isn’t automatically number 1? Well, it is a very short coaster at only 30 seconds long and over the years has gotten rough. However those are completely understandable when you have a hydraulic launch from 0-128 miles per hour in 3.5 seconds. This in itself is completely insane and on top of that you climb up a 456 ft tall top hat element making it the tallest in the world. However these records are soon to be broken, and if you would like to learn more I suggest researching Falcons Flight. This coaster is absolutely insane and deserves to be in the top 5.

Coaster Details

  • Name: Kingda Ka
  • Location: Six Flags Great Adventure in Jackson, NJ
  • Make: Intamin
  • Model: Accelerator Strata Model
  • Opened: 2005
  • Height: 456 ft
  • Length: 3,118 ft
  • Max Speed: 128 mph
  • Inversions: 0
  • Angle of Decent: 90.0°
  • Duration: 0:28
